Concussions in the NFL

The NFL has been dedicated to lowering concussion rates but is there progress?

By Jacob Hunting

The National Football League in the last 20 years has owned up to its largest medical issue; the rise of concussions and Chronic Traumatic Encephalopathy or CTE. The NFL has rolled out different attempted solutions, everything from rule changes designed to protect quarterbacks to new helmets, but have all their efforts actually resulted in reduced concussions?
